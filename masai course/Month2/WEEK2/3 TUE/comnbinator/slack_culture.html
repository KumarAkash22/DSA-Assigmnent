Working and thriving

We want people to thrive at Slack; we believe you do your best work when you feel your best. Here are just a few benefits we're proud to provide our team with.

Learning & development

Receiving an annual allowance for professional development, plus a monthly bonus you can use towards anythings. That means anything :a gym membership,your pet sitter, a new work look.Whatever matter most to you.

Health & wellness

Free medical, dental care and eye care for you and your family-Slack pays 100% of your premiums.

Parental leave

A lot of us have families, of all sorts of shapes and sizes, and we welcome each new addition with generous parental leave and "bonding leave".

Happier weekdays

A culture light on meetings (or thats's our aim, at least) with guest speakers and opportunities to get to know your team better through events like weekly catered lunches ab=nd breakfasts, Thursday socials and game nights. But we don't stayin the office 24/7.
